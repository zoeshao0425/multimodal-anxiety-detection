Patient: Yeah, I just feel really sad most of the time. I don't enjoy things that I used to like doing. I'm always tired and I have trouble sleeping at night.

Patient: It's been going on for a few weeks now.

Doctor: Have you experienced any changes in your appetite or weight?

Patient: Yeah, I haven't been eating as much as I usually do. I've lost a few pounds.

Doctor: Have you had any thoughts of hurting yourself or others?

Patient: No, I haven't had any thoughts like that.

Patient: Yeah, I've been having trouble focusing at work. It's been affecting my productivity.

Patient: Yeah, I feel like I'm not good enough and that everything I do is wrong.

Doctor: Have you noticed any changes in your energy level or motivation?

Patient: I feel really tired all the time and I don't have any motivation to do anything.

Doctor: Based on what you've told me, it sounds like you may be experiencing symptoms of depression. I'm going to diagnose you with major depressive disorder.
